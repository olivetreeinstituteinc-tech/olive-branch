How to Play

  1. Pick a category, or leave it on Surprise Me, then tap the card to draw.
  2. Answer out loud, one word counts, then pick who goes next.
  3. Not answering? Think of one question to ask once they're done.
  4. No wrong answers, tap Pass any time. Gold Wildcard? Follow what it says instead.

Tips for Grown-Ups

  • Let the youngest go first, it sets the tone that every answer counts.
  • Answer honestly yourself sometimes, kids open up more when they see it.
  • If someone goes quiet, offer the pass for them. Don't wait for them to ask.
